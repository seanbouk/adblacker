Ad Blacker — Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 11, 2026

Overview

Ad Blacker is a Chrome extension that blacks out advertisements on web pages. It is designed with privacy as a core principle: the extension operates entirely within your browser and does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Data Collection

Ad Blacker does not collect any data. Specifically:

  • No personal information is collected, stored, or shared.
  • No browsing history or page content is recorded.
  • No analytics or telemetry data is gathered.
  • No cookies are set by the extension.

Third-Party Services

Ad Blacker does not use any third-party services, APIs, or tracking tools. All processing happens locally in your browser.

Permissions

The extension requests only the minimum permissions needed to detect and hide advertisements on web pages. No data ever leaves your device.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the revised version will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact

If you have questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please open an issue on the GitHub repository.
